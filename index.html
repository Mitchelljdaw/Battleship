Introduction

Battle ship is a childhood favortie game of many of us.
The internet has provided us a much easier way to play the game without having to buy and carry the clunky board around.

Game play

  1. First select where on the game board you would like to place your battleships.
  2. Once you have selected spots for each battleship. Click 'start'
  3. You will then have an empty game board show up next to your game board that will represent the game board of your opponant
  4. Click on any spot on your opponants game board trying to guess where he placed his ships
  5. If you guess correctly then that spot on the gameboard will turn red.
  6. Continue to guess until you have guessed all the spots of your oponants battleships. And therefore sending them to the depths of the sea!
