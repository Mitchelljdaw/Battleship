Introduction

Battle ship is a childhood favortie game of many of us.
The internet has provided us a much easier way to play the game without having to buy and carry the clunky board around.

Game play

  1. Enter your name in the Name text box and hit enter.
  2. Cick on the start game to have pieces placed on board and to start the game.
  3. It'll be your turn first so click anywhere on the opponants board to make a guess.
  4. If you guess correctly then that spot on the gameboard will turn red. If you are correct it'll turn black.
  5. Continue to guess until you have guessed all the spots of your oponants battleships. And therefore sending them to the depths of the sea!
  6. Once you won you win count will be increased and you can see how many times you have won and how any times the computer hs won. on that bottom of the page. If you would like to have a fresh start click the clear button and it'll reset yours and the opponants wins to zero.
